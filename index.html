About

I'm a third-year Robotics and Automation Engineering student at the Madras Institute of Technology, currently holding a CGPA of 8.99/10. My work so far splits across three areas that keep feeding into each other.

On the hardware side, I've done electrical wiring and assembly on real industrial machine tools — profile grinders, lathes, surface grinders — and spent two weeks inside a procurement team learning how oxy-fuel, plasma, and laser cutting equipment actually gets sourced. On the personal-project side, I build small autonomous systems: an obstacle-avoiding AGV, a self-balancing robot running PID control, and circuits designed in KiCad.

I apply SolidWorks, Python, and OpenCV to problems that extend beyond the screen into working physical systems.
